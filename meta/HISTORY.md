# History

Notable events in production.

**2024-09 to 2025-07:** Shadows Dark & Deep  
Started as Shadowdark house rules and expanded to D&D support. Modular classes with specialties and talents to produce archetypes, Arc/Div/Pri spell lists, magical consequences, injuries, color of your purse. Some Shadow of the Demon Lord ideas. Soon, seeking D&D compatibility across editions, especially 3.5e, 5e, and PF.

**2025-07 to 2026-01:** Shadowjack  
Restarting with Knave 2e + Shadowjack, but then targeted 2e monsters and 2e spell compatibility.  Ends up merging (at a high level) previous Heroes of Old and SD&D work. Fairly happy with it, but still unclear what exactly I'm aiming for.

**2026-03:** Hack20 process  
After reading a blog post about changelogs and project management structure, I started thinking about my process.  Combined with a realization that I'm a decent rules editors but not a good rules creator (despite my obsessive drive to do so), I wondered if I might be able to use goals and process as a way to shore up some of my design weakness.  Also, maybe it was time to accept that I only build D&D/d20 clones - my own hack of the rules.

**2026-03-13:** Repo created  
On a Friday (the 13th) night, recovering from a migraine, I created the repo and started setting up design machinery.

**2026-03-14:** Removed changelog  
Already from the first few commits, I could see I'd erred too far in the other direction of unnecessary structure for a solo project.  It would take some tweaking to get that right and refine the [PROCESS](PROCESS.md).
